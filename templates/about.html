Built for teams that move

AwayClone creates durable, functional travel gear designed to help small teams and businesses travel smarter. We focus on quality, simple design, and service that scales with your company.

Our products are engineered to last, backed by clear warranties and business-friendly policies for bulk orders and consistent support.

Mission and values

Thoughtful design

We make purposeful travel essentials that solve real problems, not just look good. Each product is built with function-first engineering, clean aesthetics, and predictable performance.

Reliability

Business travel is unforgiving. We design for durability, simple repairs, and clear warranties so teams can focus on work, not gear breakdowns.

Transparent business terms

We offer straightforward pricing, bulk discounts, and dedicated support for company accounts so procurement is simple and repeatable.

Sustainability and materials

We prioritize long-lived materials and responsible manufacturing. Where possible we select recycled fabrics, reduce unnecessary packaging, and optimize logistics to lower our carbon footprint. For transparency, we publish material and repair information for each product.

  • Recycled fabrics: Select styles use post-consumer recycled polyester.
  • Repairable parts: Replaceable wheels, handles, and interior components.
  • Minimal packaging: Recyclable boxes and reduced plastic where possible.

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes. Bulk pricing and business accounts are available. Contact sales for custom quotes and lead times.

What is your returns policy?

We offer a 30-day return window on unused items. Damaged items are handled under warranty; please contact support for details.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping is 3–7 business days depending on destination. Business orders may have longer lead times depending on quantity and customization.
